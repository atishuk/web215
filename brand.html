Brand Guide

This summer, my husband decided to make a wood workshop in our garage. We insulated, painted the walls and floor, installed an air conditioner in the garage. My husband bought equipment and, now, is making cabinets for his workshop. Also, he has already made several cutting boards and wood-epoxy wall decors. So I decided to make a brand for our wood workshop.

Brand name is Wood Charm.

For this brand, I have chosen "wooden" palette with yellow, brown, gray, and black colors.

Catchphrases

  • Beauty of natural wood for your home.
  • We create custom handmade wooden decor.
  • Best cutting boards for you.
